Project Summary  

The Guelph Public Health Center has been designed to accommodate a range of private, semi-private and public spaces. Modern clinic rooms will assist with the provision of comprehensive client care: travel clinics, sexual health and testing, dental clinics, prenatal healthcare, and immunizations.

+VG architects has provided WDGPH with high level functional building designs with full and complete adherence to public health safety protocol and standards. Reception area and public waiting areas, at the main entry, are fully visible to enforce intuitive navigation and security. Calming, inviting and durable finishes were selected for longevity, ease of maintenance and healthy atmospheric environment.

This facility incorporates a transparent design allowing light to go deep into the centre of the building. Thermally insulated curtain wall systems surround the building perimeter along with a large translucent skylight. Light passes through to a three story atrium which is enhanced with rows of highly effective LED pendant light fixtures to compensate for the evening and night time use. Key features in the design that help achieve this include:

  1. Energy-efficient LED and T5 lighting
  2. Low voltage lighting control system and occupancy sensors for interior lighting control
  3. Photocell control of exterior lighting
  4. Plug load control in office areas
