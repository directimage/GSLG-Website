GS Lighting Group features the Masri O Architects Main Office project for the months of June and July. This project, located at 609 Kumpf Drive, Waterloo, introduces a variety of lighting elements to create a modern and inviting lighting space that compliments Masri O's architectural design elements. Lighting has been installed in three main areas of the building; the lobby showcases the Eureka Cycle LED, the boardroom uses the Metalumen RML Caledonia and the office area has the Metalumen Flex FS4F. Each fixture was installed with design, productivity and energy conservation in mind.
