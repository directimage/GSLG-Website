Automotive Dealership LED Lighting Initiative

GS Lighting Group is pleased to introduce our Automobile LED Dealership Program with the completion of several local dealerships. We are very pleased that our leadership position with our manufacturing partners provides industry leading LED product that gives us the opportunity to design and supply LED lighting for; exterior parking lot applications, showroom, office and service areas. These designs and products provide LED lighting solutions that out perform traditional fixtures and provide energy savings that can exceed over 60%.

Whether it is new construction or retrofit, the ROI (return on investment) with these LED solutions can show a 2-4 year payback. Our involvement with General Motors, Ford, Toyota, Audi, Volkswagen, Hyundai, Kia and others have given us a leadership role in this market.
