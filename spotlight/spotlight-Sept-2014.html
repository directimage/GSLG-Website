Project Summary  

Stratford's newest luxury hotel is just steps from world-renowned Stratford Festival theatre and some of the nation's finest restaurants. Set on six and a half acres, The Bruce Hotel offers unparalleled service in the heart of Stratford. The Bruce Hotel in Stratford, Ontario opened its doors this summer to serve the dedicated patrons who travel to Stratford for the Festival Theatre and all that Stratford has to offer, and are looking for a hotel facility that offers the highest standards.

The Bruce is a beautifully designed boutique hotel, offering 25 luxurious rooms with all the facilities to cater to the most demanding traveller/tourist.
